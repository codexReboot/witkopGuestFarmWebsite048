2. Introduction

This manual provides guidance for requesting access to information held by Isanti Guest Farm under the Promotion of Access to Information Act (PAIA). Isanti Guest Farm provides guest accommodation and recreational services in Limpopo, South Africa.

3. Records Held by Isanti Guest Farm

  • Business registration and ownership documents
  • Financial and accounting records (internal use)
  • Booking and accommodation records
  • Personnel and supplier information (internal use)
  • General correspondence and marketing materials

These records are used for operational purposes only and are not shared externally except where required by law.

5. Procedure for Access to Information

To request access to any non-public record:

  • Submit a written request using the prescribed PAIA form to the Information Officer via email.
  • Include your name, contact details, and sufficient detail describing the records requested.
  • Requests will be processed as required by law. Access will be granted only if justified under the PAIA.

6. Fees

Fees may be applicable for copies or postage as determined by the Information Regulator.

7. Automatically Available Information

  • General Website content (about, accommodation, activities, gallery)
  • Publicly available social media posts and Google reviews

8. POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act) Statement

  • Isanti Guest Farm does not collect personal information via this Website.
  • Contact information received voluntarily (e.g., via email or phone) is used only to reply to enquiries or process bookings.
  • No personal information is shared or sold to third parties.
